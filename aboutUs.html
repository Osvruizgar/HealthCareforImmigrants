ABOUT HEALTH FOR ALL

Health for All Mission

Health for All understands that many immigrants in america, can't afford or be eligiable for healthcare. It is our goal to provide services and products for immigrants without insurance and without the outrages cost that come with healthcare.

Though our main priority is short term, we plan to fight for states to give healthcare to the undocument and provide more support to immigrants in low-income communities.

We have partnered with an organization that doubles the amount of each purchase and returns it back to us to keep up with costs.

After her evaluation, she was told to have a hip replacement surgery on both hips or she wont be able to walk ever again. Her total cost was $160,000.
